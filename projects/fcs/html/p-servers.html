Servers

FCS is certified to sell and service the top three midrange computer servers and storage servers in rack mount, tower and blade configuration.

IBM:

IBM has consistently won awards for making the most reliable, highest performing midrange servers in the industry and FCS has won awards from IBM for its outstanding sales and service for IBM xSeries Servers. FCS offers the full range of IBM xSeries servers, Blade servers and the DS line of storage servers.

HP:

HP consistently offers high quality, state of the art servers and storage. FCS sells and supports the entire line of HP Proliant DL, ML and BL servers and storage.

DELL:

DELL has earned a seat at the table of the top three servers by offering high quality, cost effective server solutions. FCS is a proud certified reseller of DELL PowerEdge servers to both the education and private sectors.

STORAGE:

FCS has extensive experience with iSCSI, DAS and Fiber Channel SAN and NAS storage devices. Let FCS design a cost effective storage solution and demystify the confusion surrounding storage.

Remote Sites:

The Cisco system is designed to work easily with teleworkers working from home, hotel or a small remote office just as if they were in the same building. It is also easy to network Cisco PBXs together from different offices into one large, seamless system.

Server Virtualization:

The advantages of server virtualization continue to grow. FCS is a certified partner with VMware and has been installing and servicing VMware virtualized servers for many years. Today, it doesn't make sense to stand up a server that is not virtualized and VMware is currently the best.
