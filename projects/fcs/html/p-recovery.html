Disaster Recovery & Backup

FCS provides 24 x 7 disaster recovery and back up...

Symantec System Recovery 2011:

Semantec System Recovery 2011 delivers fast and reliable system recovery to help you minimize downtime and meeet recovery time objectives with confidence. Quickly restore physical and virtual systems in munutes.

Symantec's BESR:

Backup Exec System Recovery known as BESR is designed to offer advanced system protection by delivering a rapid, reliable approach to system recovery. It also provides a nonintrusive method for backing up mission-critical systems in organizations of any size.

SAN Backup and Recovery:

A SAN typically consists of multiple servers, online storage (disk), and offline storage (tape or ptical), all of which are connected to a Fibre Challen switch or hub - usually a switch.

Barracuda Backup:

Barracuda Backup Service is an affordable data backup solution that provides full local data backups with several options for offsite data replication. It provides complete control over where data is stored. Barracuda offers a off-site full system backup, which backs up to the cloud, allows companies to back up their data both locally and offsite without the hassle of tapes or other removable media. It is a quick, simple way to protect data in the event of a disaster.

Tandberg Data:

Tandberg Data offers a complete range of tape drives, autoloaders, libraries and media, disk systems and deduplication software, designed to meet the backup, recovery and archive requirements of education, businesses and non-profit around the world.
